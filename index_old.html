"Secure Biometric Recognition" Project Page

This webpage introduces a series of research papers on "Secure Biometric Recognition" published by members of C&A Laboratory@Hanyang University since 2021.

Our primary focus is on facial recognition systems, which are widely deployed in practice; however, we are also broadening our scope to include other biometrics, such as voice.
